A portion of every transaction – tournament fees, marketplace sales, virtual card payments – is funnelled into a buyback‑and‑burn mechanism. These funds are used to purchase $FOOT from the open market and permanently remove it from circulation. This relentless reduction in supply continues until the circulating supply reaches just **100 million $FOOT**, creating long‑term scarcity and value for holders.

### From Simulated to Real

During the Early Mining Phase, all $FOOT balances are simulated. They exist as numbers in your device's local storage. When the Token Generation Event arrives, a snapshot is taken of every user's balance. Those simulated tokens are then converted into real, on‑chain $FOOT tokens on the Solana blockchain. You claim yours simply by logging in with your seedphrase or restoring from a backup. It's zero‑friction earning today, with real ownership tomorrow.

## The Full METAFOOT Experience

The mining app you see right now is the foundation. Here's how the entire ecosystem will unfold.

### 🔐 Your Wallet, Your Rules

When you first open METAFOOT, you create a wallet. A 12‑word seedphrase is generated on your device using cryptographically secure randomness. You set a password, and that seedphrase is encrypted and stored safely in your browser's local storage. A unique Account ID is derived from your seedphrase – this is your identity on the network.

You can log back in with your password, your Account ID plus password, a full encrypted backup file, or simply your seedphrase. Because the seedphrase is the ultimate key, nobody can lock you out or ban you. You are the only custodian of your account. This is true self‑sovereignty.

### ⛏️ Daily Mining

Every 24 hours, you can claim your daily $FOOT reward by tapping the glowing mining circle. The reward rate depends on the total number of active miners – a built‑in halving mechanism that ensures fairness:

| Active Miners | Daily Reward |
|---------------|--------------|
| 0 – 14,999    | 4 $FOOT      |
| 15,000 – 99,999 | 2 $FOOT    |
| 100,000 – 499,999 | 1 $FOOT  |
| 500,000 – 999,999 | 0.5 $FOOT |
| 1,000,000+     | 0.25 $FOOT   |

This system rewards early believers while preserving value for everyone. The total airdrop capped at 50 million $FOOT. A live countdown timer shows you exactly when your next claim is ready.

### 🏋️ Player Training

Every user has a digital footballer with six core attributes – Speed, Shoot, Power, Intelligence, Brilliance, and Accuracy. You spend $FOOT to improve these stats. However, the cost per point doubles every 20 levels, creating a natural balance:

| Current Level | Cost per Point |
|---------------|----------------|
| 0 – 19        | 0.3 $FOOT      |
| 20 – 39       | 0.6 $FOOT      |
| 40 – 59       | 1.2 $FOOT      |
| 60 – 79       | 2.4 $FOOT      |
| 80 – 99       | 4.8 $FOOT      |

This progressive curve prevents whales from buying instant dominance and rewards dedication over time. Your player's total "worth" is the sum of all $FOOT spent on training – a transparent valuation that will underpin the future player marketplace.

### 👥 Referral Programme

Your Account ID is also your referral code. Share it with friends; when they enter your code, you both earn a reward. The reward amount scales with the halving schedule, ensuring incentives remain fair as the network grows. Pending rewards are tracked in the app and will be credited at the Token Generation Event.

### 📱 Social Tasks

Stay engaged and earn extra $FOOT by completing simple social tasks that refresh every 30 minutes:
*   Visit the official Telegram channel – earn 0.6 $FOOT.
*   Visit the official X (Twitter) account – earn 0.6 $FOOT.

Cooldown timers keep everything transparent, and the app uses deep links to open the respective apps smoothly.

### 📱 Social Feed (Coming Soon)

Imagine a social feed that works like the early days of Twitter – purely chronological, with no hidden algorithms. Your reach is determined by a transparent, stake‑based tier system. The more $FOOT you hold and stake, the more people see your posts, but everyone starts with a generous free allocation. No pay‑to‑boost, no shadow banning, no opaque curation. Just honest, community‑driven conversation about football.

### ⚔️ PvP Arena & Tournaments

Challenge other players to real‑time football matches. Wager $FOOT on the outcome – the winner takes the pot, minus a small ecosystem fee that goes towards the buyback‑and‑burn. Climb the seasonal leaderboard, earn exclusive badges, and prove your training regimen is the best.

Managers will be able to form teams of trained players and enter four‑tier leagues:
*   **$FOOT Survival** – Entry level, top 3 advance.
*   **$FOOT Mission League** – Premier league equivalent.
*   **$FOOT Classic League** – International club competition.
*   **$FOOT Elite League** – The ultimate championship.

Prize pools are split fairly: 70% to the players, 30% to the manager – rule enforced transparently.

### 🛒 Player Marketplace

Once the game launches, a decentralised marketplace will allow managers to buy and sell trained player avatars. The baseline value of each player is determined by the total $FOOT invested in their attributes – a clear, fair valuation that gives early miners instant liquidity. Scout talent, build your dream team, and trade like a real football club.

### 💳 $FOOT Virtual Card & Physical Shops

One of the most ambitious parts of the METAFOOT roadmap is the $FOOT Virtual Card. It's a real‑world debit card funded directly by your $FOOT balance. Use it online, or walk into a METAFOOT‑branded retail store and pay with your phone. No currency exchange, no third‑party processor – just pure ecosystem utility. This closes the loop between the digital and physical worlds.

### 🎮 The Full Game

The full METAFOOT game will be a realistic 3D football manager experience, first on mobile and later on PC. The mining phase is your head start – train your avatar now, stack $FOOT, and be ready to dominate from day one.
